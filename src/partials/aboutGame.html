About the game

Water Splash" is a vibrant match-3 puzzle game where players journey through a colorful water world, aiming to match colorful water balloons to clear challenging levels. Set in a charming environment with Oris the Otter, your goal is to help him defeat the crocodile and bring water back to his village. With over 1,000 levels each offering unique challenges and gameplay mechanics, players will enjoy an immersive experience with rich graphics and interactive water effects that bring each level to life. Strategic gameplay is crucial as you use special water balloons, combine multiple power-ups, and solve puzzles to progress. The game also integrates social features, allowing players to compete against friends and climb the leaderboards in weekly tournaments.

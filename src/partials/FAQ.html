FAQ

How do I get more lives?

Lives refill over time, or you can request them from friends.

Can I play offline?

The game requires an internet connection for some features but can be played offline otherwise.

How to use power-ups effectively?

Use power-ups to clear difficult tiles or save them for complicated levels.

Are there in-app purchases?

Yes, but they are optional and not required to progress.

How to get high scores?

Combine multiple combos and use power-ups strategically.

Is there a way to sync game progress across devices?

Yes, by connecting to social media accounts.

What happens if I uninstall the game?

Your progress can be lost unless synced online.

Are there any rewards for playing daily?

Yes, daily bonuses and special events offer rewards.

Can I challenge my friends?

Yes, invite friends for more fun and competition.

Is customer support available?

Yes, through in-game settings and the support website.
